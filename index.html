Help & System Introduction

Learn all features of DeutschSphere

Welcome to DeutschSphere! This ultra-premium platform seamlessly integrates clinical memory science with interactive immersion boards to accelerate your German acquisition.

01.

FSRS-5 Spaced Repetition

Every card utilizes the state-of-the-art FSRS-5 (Free Spaced Repetition Scheduler) algorithm. Rate your memory recall using four options: Again, Hard, Good, or Easy. The scheduler calculates your stability and optimal review intervals dynamically. Responsive touch swipe gestures (swipe left for Hard, swipe right for Good) are supported on mobile.

02.

Keyboard Shortcuts & Fluid Navigation

Flashcard Arena
  • Space Reveal translation & examples
  • 1 - 4 Rate FSRS recall rating
  • ← / → Navigate previous / next card
  • L Toggle marked as learned (advances deck)
  • V Pronounce German word via TTS
Interactive Modes
  • 1 - 9 Select scrambled word chips
  • Enter Submit assembled answer
  • Backspace Undo/remove last selected chip
03.

Interactive Immersion Lab (NLP)

Deconstruct sentences inside the Immersion Lab. Click on any parsed word token in the text grid to reveal its Part-of-Speech (POS) category, CEFR status, active FSRS spacing records, hear its TTS pronunciation, and instantly import it as a custom card in your review deck.

04.

Suffix Grammar Contextualizer

The smart parser detects typical German noun endings (such as -ung, -heit, or -keit) and renders an inline lightbulb tipped drawer showing the corresponding gender association rules (such as die for nouns ending in -ung). Adjectives and verbs are protected from rules pollution.

05.

Phonetik-Spiegel (Speech Pronunciation Coach)

Perfect your accent using our high-fidelity Speech Recognition engine. The pure-JS Kölner Phonetik algorithm scores your spoken input against target pronunciations. If you fail to pronounce tricky German phonemes like ö, ü, ch, sch, or r, an interactive mouth positioning drawer pops open with detailed anatomical illustrations and tongue positioning guides.

06.

Backup Sync & Profile Recovery

Maintain complete control of your progress data. Import or export your progress profile instantly using a Base64-encrypted copy/paste Sync Key, or backup and restore raw JSON profile files directly.

07.

Offline Sandboxing & Multi-User Isolation

DeutschSphere is 100% client-side with no centralized database servers. Your learning progress is sandboxed inside your browser's private IndexedDB storage. Multiple users can access the same URL independently; their learning records will remain perfectly isolated on their respective devices.

Run Offline Locally: You can download or clone this entire project repository to your device and double-click index.html (or host it with any simple local web server, or install it as a PWA) to study 100% offline without an internet connection.
